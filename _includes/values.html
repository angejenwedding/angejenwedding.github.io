Values

As we get ready to share this important day with you, we wanted to share some other things that are important to us.

  • We believe that America was founded on white supremacy, and that it is the responsibility of all of us who have benefited to help elevate those who have been harmed.
  • We believe that black lives matter.
  • We believe that no human is illegal and that family separation is a crime against humanity.
  • We believe that people of all genders should have the entire range of human experience available to them.
  • We believe that pregnancy should be a choice, that all young people should be taught comprehensive sex ed and that birth control should be free and easily available.
  • We believe that everyone should be paid equally for equal work, and that all people should be paid a living wage.
  • We believe that LGBTQ identities are valid and that their presence in society is a benefit.
  • We believe that food, clean water, housing, education and healthcare are basic human rights.
  • We believe that diversity is a strength, and that actively seeking out diversity over multiple dimensions is crucial.
  • We believe that equity is more important than equality.
  • We believe that representation matters.
  • We understand science - and science is true whether you believe or not!
